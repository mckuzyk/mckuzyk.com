# About Me
I'm Mark, a data scientist based in Spokane, WA.

I did my PhD in physics at the University of Oregon, spent a few years as a
postdoc at Oregon and Duke working on quantum computing hardware, then made the
jump to industry.

Since then I've been building ML systems for patent analytics and financial
data: deep learning architectures, entity resolution pipelines,
human-in-the-loop clustering, LLM-powered text processing at scale. My main
tools are Python, PyTorch, and SQL, though the full list is long.

Outside of work I like to tinker with projects at the intersection of math,
data, and whatever I'm currently curious about — which is mostly what you'll
find on this blog. I'm currently open to new opportunities — feel free to reach
out on LinkedIn.
